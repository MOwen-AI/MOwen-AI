# Mary Owen
### Conversation & Interaction Designer | LLM UX

I design conversational experiences for LLM products that are reliable, clear, and safe to use.  
I turn messy model behavior into interaction patterns teams can document, test, and iterate on.

---

## What I Do

- **Persona Architecture** — building psychologically grounded AI characters with stable identity logic, adaptive tone systems, and ethical safety frameworks
- **Conversational UX** — turn design, clarifying questions, error recovery, and interaction flows for LLM products
- **Safety UX** — refusal design, guardrail logic, sensitive topic handling, and user trust
- **Behavioral Research** — hands-on model testing, drift analysis, and case studies drawn from real interaction data
